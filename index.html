About Abdulla Carpentry EST

We provide interior fit-out, renovation, joinery and finishing services for clients looking for practical, professionally managed, and well-finished spaces in Dubai.

Abdulla Carpentry EST works across residential and commercial sectors, supporting projects such as villa renovation, apartment upgrades, office fit-out, retail interiors, salon fit-out, gym fit-out, painting, wall paneling, furniture making, upholstery, kitchen remodeling and bathroom renovation.
